Our Services

Everything you need — beyond just buying a car.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Automotive Services

At LankaDrive, we don't just sell cars — we support you throughout your entire ownership journey with a full suite of professional services.

🏦

Vehicle Leasing Assistance

Owning your dream car is more affordable than you think. We work with Sri Lanka's leading banks and financial institutions to secure the most competitive leasing rates tailored to your income and lifestyle.

  • Partnerships with 10+ leading banks & finance companies
  • Low down-payment options available
  • Flexible repayment terms (12 – 84 months)
  • Quick approval process — as fast as 24 hours
  • Dedicated finance advisor for every customer
🛡️

Motor Insurance

Protect your investment with the best motor insurance plans available in Sri Lanka. Our insurance specialists compare policies across multiple providers to find you the right cover at the right price.

  • Comprehensive & third-party insurance
  • Agency repair coverage available
  • Instant quote comparison service
  • Claims assistance and support
  • Renewal reminders and management
📋

Vehicle Registration & Documentation

Skip the queues and paperwork. Our dedicated registration team handles the entire process of registering your vehicle with the Department of Motor Traffic (DMT) on your behalf.

  • Full DMT registration handling
  • Revenue licence processing
  • Emission test coordination
  • Transfer of ownership documentation
  • Number plate procurement
🚗

Test Drive Booking

Experience the car before you commit. Schedule a test drive at our Colombo showroom or, in select cases, at a location of your convenience. No pressure — just the joy of driving.

  • Available for all vehicles in our inventory
  • Flexible time slots: morning, afternoon & weekend
  • Expert guidance from our sales team during the drive
  • Book online via our Contact page
🌏

Custom Vehicle Sourcing

Can't find your perfect car in our current inventory? We source specific makes, models, colours, and specifications directly from Japan, Korea, and other global markets on request.

  • Direct access to auction houses in Japan
  • Detailed vehicle inspection reports before shipping
  • Transparent import cost breakdown
  • Average delivery: 6–10 weeks
🔧

After-Sales & Warranty Support

Our commitment to you doesn't end at the sale. We provide ongoing after-sales support to keep your vehicle in peak condition and help manage any warranty claims.

  • Warranty registration & claim assistance
  • Referrals to authorised service centres
  • Periodic vehicle checkup reminders
  • 24/7 roadside assistance coordination
💎

Free Vehicle Valuation

Thinking of trading in your old vehicle? We provide free, no-obligation market valuations conducted by our certified automotive experts to give you the best trade-in price.

  • 100% free assessment — no hidden charges
  • Certified automotive expert evaluation
  • Market-rate pricing based on current demand
  • Instant quote provided on the day
🏢

Corporate Fleet Solutions

We provide tailored automotive solutions for businesses. Whether you need two vehicles or twenty, our fleet team will structure a cost-effective package for your organisation.

  • Volume-based pricing discounts
  • Fleet leasing & financing packages
  • Dedicated corporate account manager
  • Fleet insurance management
