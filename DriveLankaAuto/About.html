About LankaDrive

Two decades of automotive excellence in the heart of Colombo.

Our Story

Colombo's Most Trusted Auto Dealer

Founded in 2005, LankaDrive Auto Sales (Pvt) Ltd has grown from a small used-vehicle lot in Colombo 09 into one of Sri Lanka's most reputable automotive dealers. With over two decades of expertise, we have built our reputation on honesty, quality, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

We are authorised to source vehicles directly from Japan and other global markets, ensuring that every car in our showroom meets the highest standards of safety and reliability. Whether you're searching for a factory-fresh luxury sedan, a fuel-efficient Japanese hybrid, or a budget-friendly pre-owned vehicle, we have something for every driver and every budget.

Our team of certified automotive specialists, finance advisors, and customer service professionals are united by a single goal: to make your car-buying journey as smooth, transparent, and enjoyable as possible. From the moment you step into our showroom to the day you drive away, LankaDrive is with you every step of the way.
